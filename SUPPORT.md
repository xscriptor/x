<h2 id="before-opening-an-issue" align="center">Before Opening an Issue</h2>

<ul>
  <li>Confirm you are on the latest <code>main</code> commit.</li>
  <li>Include your distribution/version (or WSL details), shell, and reproduction steps.</li>
  <li>Share relevant logs and the exact command used to run <code>x.sh</code> or setup scripts.</li>
  <li>Describe expected behavior and observed behavior clearly.</li>
</ul>

<h2 id="response-expectations" align="center">Response Expectations</h2>

<p>
  Maintainers review reports as time allows. Well-scoped reports with reproducible steps are prioritized.
</p>
