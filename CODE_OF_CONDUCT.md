<h1 align="center">Code of Conduct</h1>

<p align="center">
  The X community is committed to a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free environment.
</p>

<hr />

<h2 id="our-standards" align="center">Our Standards</h2>

<ul>
  <li>Use clear, respectful, and constructive communication.</li>
  <li>Accept feedback professionally and focus on technical outcomes.</li>
  <li>Support contributors of all backgrounds and experience levels.</li>
  <li>Prioritize collaboration and shared learning.</li>
</ul>

<h2 id="unacceptable-behavior" align="center">Unacceptable Behavior</h2>

<ul>
  <li>Harassment, discrimination, hate speech, or personal attacks.</li>
  <li>Trolling, intimidation, or sustained hostile interactions.</li>
  <li>Sharing private information without consent.</li>
  <li>Any behavior that creates an unsafe environment.</li>
</ul>

<h2 id="scope" align="center">Scope</h2>

<p>
  This policy applies to project spaces, including issues, pull requests, and related community channels.
</p>

<h2 id="reporting" align="center">Reporting</h2>

<p>
  Report conduct issues to <a href="mailto:x@xscriptor.com">x@xscriptor.com</a>. Reports are handled confidentially.
</p>

<hr />

<p align="center"><b>Participation in this repository implies agreement with this Code of Conduct.</b></p>
